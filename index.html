Finn is a twelve year old boy living in the magical world of Ooo. He fights monsters and saves pricesses and hangs out with his best friend, Jake the Dog. Finn values bravery, strength, and helping those in need. He and Jake meet many strange creatures during their adventures, but they always have each others' backs.

Finn is a character in Adventure Time, a TV show.

A list of adjectives that describe Finn:

  • Brave
  • Strong
  • Helpful
